Pencil E-Learning Solution

Pencil is a fully functioning off-the-shelf Learning Management System designed for corporates and used by many of our clients. Apart from the traditional courses, it features social learning tools which integrates learning spaces and events together with traditional courses providing a blended learning environment. The platform comes with a various tools to help you build your courses, arrange materials, create learning paths and utilize a huge array of resources and activities.

Classroom Management System

The smart classroom classroom is a collaborative platform built to allow students and instructors to communicate with one another instantly. It rides on technologies like Jabber (same technology as GoogleTalk) to provide an instance messaging platform for users to collaborate. Instructors are armed with powerful tools which can bring out interactive whiteboards, load Powerpoints, observe student’s screens, lock their tablets or computers etc. This platform works on all three platforms with some limitations which are platform dependent.
